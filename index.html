About Me

Hi, I’m Doug. I am an early-career engineer who graduated from Iowa State University with a Master’s in Electrical Engineering in May, 2023. My focus was on microwave electronics but I also put a heavy emphasis on analog integrated circuit design. Currently, I work for IBM as a hardware developer.

In my free time, I work on a variety of projects. My projects usually focus on 3D printing and electronics, but I am trying to expand my knowledge. This site is where I discuss my latest experiences and projects.
